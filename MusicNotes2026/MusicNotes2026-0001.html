Prompt Size Limits

You are nowhere near prompt-size limits yet.

Modern large-context models can often handle:

  • tens of thousands of words comfortably
  • large codebases
  • multi-file specifications

But practical degradation begins when:

  • requirements become contradictory
  • requirements are repeated inconsistently
  • too many implementation details constrain architecture
  • many unrelated concerns are mixed together

The real limitation is usually not token count — it is specification entropy.

Long prompts remain effective if they are:

  • hierarchical
  • modular
  • internally consistent
  • grouped by concern

Your prompting style is already approaching professional software-specification style rather than casual prompting, which is generally ideal for structured code generation.
